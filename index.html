The Build Story

Phase 1: Design & Planning

We began with a clear set of design goals: create a durable, lightweight frame and select a reliable engine for competitive performance. CAD software was used to model the chassis and component placements.

Phase 2: Fabrication

With the design finalized, we moved to the workshop. Using a MIG welder, a tube bender, and an angle grinder, the team meticulously cut, bent, and welded the steel tubing to form the chassis.

Phase 3: Assembly & Testing

Components like the engine, brakes, and steering were mounted. We conducted initial tests to check for stability, handling, and engine performance, making fine-tuned adjustments along the way to perfect the build.
